Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

SpotBookie ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our booking platform.

1. Information We Collect

For Business Owners:

  • Account information (name, email, password)
  • Business information (shop name, address, services offered)
  • Payment information (processed securely via Stripe)

For Clients Booking Appointments:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Booking details (services, dates, times)
  • Payment information for deposits (processed via Stripe — we never store card numbers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate appointment bookings and communications
  • To send booking confirmations, reminders, and updates
  • To process payments securely
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To send review requests after completed appointments (if enabled by the business)

3. Data Security

We take security seriously:
  • All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure (SOC 2, ISO 27001 certified)
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Payment card data is handled exclusively by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant)
  • Access controls restrict data to authorized users only

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only:

  • With the business you're booking with (so they can serve you)
  • With service providers who help us operate (Stripe for payments, Resend for emails)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services. Business owners can delete client data upon request. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing communications

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@spotbookie.com

Summary: We collect only what's needed to provide booking services. Your data is encrypted and secure. We never sell your information. You can request deletion at any time.
